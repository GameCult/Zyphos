## Useful Pressure

Public reviews repeatedly frame *Semiosis* around colonists learning that plant intelligence and planetary ecology are not passive background. That pressure is directly useful for [[Ecological Bargaining]] and [[Ecological Memory Partners]].

## Translation Into This Setting

The setting should avoid making plants either wise elders or decorative networks. A mother tree can be nurturing, strategic, coercive, or frightened. A remembered ecology can reward generosity without becoming morally pure.

The useful move is agency without sentimentality.
