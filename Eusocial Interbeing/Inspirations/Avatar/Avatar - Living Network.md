## Useful Pressure

Avatar's living network idea is useful because it makes ecology a communicative and sacred infrastructure. This setting pushes harder on the political economy of that infrastructure.

## Re-engineering

In Avatar, the network often functions as spiritual ecology and planetary continuity. Here, the nearest equivalent, [[Mother Tree Networks]], can preserve memory while also creating hostage relationships.

The question becomes: what happens when the archive wants payment, and one society invents a tool to stop paying?
