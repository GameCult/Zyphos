# Sa'auei'a Nomadic Civilization

Sa'auei'a civilization is highly advanced without being sedentary in the imperial sense. It preserves knowledge through mobile families, ritual routes, ecological partners, portable craft, and sacred reproductive commons.

## Institutions

- [[Sa'auei'a Family Units]] carry law, skill, memory, and diplomacy.
- [[Breeding Grounds]] anchor infancy, infirm care, reproductive ritual, and inter-family legitimacy.
- ecological partners remember generosity and punish extraction.
- route assemblies resolve disputes when multiple families converge.

## Misread by the Empire

The [[Airawa Empire]] interprets Sa'auei'a mobility as primitiveness because the empire's own imagination has been captured by network control and fixed biological infrastructure.

This is not just arrogance. It is path dependency as a perceptual disability.
