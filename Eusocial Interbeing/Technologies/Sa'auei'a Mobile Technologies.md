# Sa'auei'a Mobile Technologies

Sa'auei'a technology is advanced, portable, repairable, and relationship-aware. It is built for movement through ecologies that remember treatment.

## Likely Forms

- collapsible shelters grown from dormant symbionts;
- route-memory instruments that encode ecological obligations;
- medical grafts maintained by breeding-ground specialists;
- scent, vibration, and pattern languages for partner species;
- portable archives split across families and living companions.

## Contrast

The [[Airawa Empire]] scales power through network capture. Sa'auei'a technologies scale trust through repeated return.
