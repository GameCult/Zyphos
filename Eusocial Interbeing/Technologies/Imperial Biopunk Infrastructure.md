# Imperial Biopunk Infrastructure

Imperial biopunk infrastructure grows from the [[Biological Memetic Engine]]. The empire builds with organs, trees, grafts, controlled parasites, caste-tuned bodies, memory nodules, pheromonal courts, and immune checkpoints.

## Uses

- administrative consensus;
- border surveillance;
- reproductive licensing;
- military coordination;
- public health framed as ideological hygiene;
- historical revision through living archives.

## Limit

The infrastructure is strongest where networks are connected, legible, and
already shaped by imperial boundary discipline. It is weaker against native
[[Nation Scale Interbeings]], [[Disconnected Tree Networks]], and the
[[Sa'auei'a Continent]], where cooperation is distributed through many local
ecological relationships rather than one continental archive.
