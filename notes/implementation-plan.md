# Implementation Plan

## Phase 1: Baseline Vault

Complete. The vault has a linked starting structure, inspiration notes, state machinery, and initial setting canon.

## Phase 2: Naming and Ontology

- Decide whether "Eusocial Interbeing" is the final setting title or only the vault title.
- Name the two continents.
- Define Airawa anatomy, reproduction, sensory world, and caste pressures.
- Define Sa'auei'a anatomy, reproduction, lifespan, family-unit roles, and mobility constraints.
- Define mother-tree biology without making the trees into human elders with bark.

## Phase 3: Systems Expansion

- Expand the Biological Memetic Engine into mechanism, limits, costs, and failure states.
- Detail imperial ministries, breeding law, military infrastructure, and denial doctrine.
- Detail Sa'auei'a route law, breeding-ground governance, ecological treaties, and mobile technologies.
- Map the Continental Contact War through several fronts.

## Phase 4: Story Hooks

- Add character-facing notes only after the ecological and political machinery can support them.
- Prefer conflicts that expose incompatible systems rather than generic invasion drama.
